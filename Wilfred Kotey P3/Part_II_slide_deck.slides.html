Effects of client type, age and gender on the frequency and length of trips¶

by Wilfred Kotey¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I wanted to look at the time most trips were taken and how it was influenced by the clientele of the bike service. I also wanted to find the relationship between age and length of the trip and also the gender and the length of trip.

Dataset Overview¶

The dataset contains 174952 rows and 21 columns for the Ford GoBike System in a bike-sharing system after 8460 data points were removed from the analysis due to missing information. The features explored included age, gender, number of trips and the duration of the trips.

Days and time of day when most trips are taken by clientele¶

A clustered bar chart of hours and the trip counts with the different users shows which time the service was mostly used and by who. It shows the time of the day when the service had very high patronage. A heatmap also shows that there were a lot more subscribers on every day of the week as compared to casual users.
